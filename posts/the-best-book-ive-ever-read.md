# The Best Book I've Ever Read

My favorite book I have ever read is [The Three Body Problem](https://www.amazon.com/Three-Body-Problem-Cixin-Liu/dp/0765382032). Of all of the books I've ever read, this has stood out as the single most impactful one.

Its a perfect blend of a sciency sci-fi plot, thought through character development, and awesome worldbuilding!

If you like:

- [The Martian](https://www.amazon.com/Martian-Andy-Weir/dp/0553418025)
- [Dune](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dune_(novel)>)

Then make sure to check out The Three Body Problem!
